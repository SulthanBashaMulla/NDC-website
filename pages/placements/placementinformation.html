About Placement Cell ‚Äì Empowering Careers, Shaping Futures

The Placement Cell of our college is dedicated to bridging the gap between education and employment by equipping students with the necessary skills, knowledge, and opportunities to excel in their careers. We take a holistic approach to career development, ensuring that our students are well-prepared to meet industry expectations and challenges.

Our Key Initiatives

1. In-Campus Training with a Dedicated Team

To enhance the employability of our students, we provide comprehensive in-campus training led by our permanent training team. This team consists of industry experts and experienced faculty members who conduct regular sessions on:

  • Soft skills development (communication, leadership, teamwork, etc.)
  • Technical and domain-specific skills
  • Aptitude and logical reasoning training
  • Resume building and interview preparation

2. Collaboration with Government & Non-Government Organizations

We partner with various government and non-government organizations like APSSDC (Andhra Pradesh State Skill Development Corporation), APITA (Andhra Pradesh Information Technology Academy), Wadhwani Foundation etc to provide specialized training programs. These collaborations help students gain:

  • Established a Employability Skill centre (ESC) by APSSDC to conduct regular training for un-employed youth and also the students in the campus.
  • The infrastructure like Laptops are provided by the APSSDC.
  • Certification courses in programming, data analytics, AI/ML, cybersecurity, and other emerging fields
  • Hands-on experience through workshops, internships, and live projects
  • Training in professional ethics, corporate culture, and workplace adaptability

3. Keeping Up with Emerging Technologies

The Placement Cell actively keeps students updated on the latest trends and innovations in the technical world. Through seminars, expert talks, webinars, and industry interactions, students get exposure to:

  • Artificial Intelligence & Machine Learning
  • Cloud Computing & Cybersecurity
  • Internet of Things (IoT) & Blockchain
  • Financial Technology (FinTech)
  • Bioinformatics & Pharmaceutical advancements

4. Industry-Specific Placement Training

Before companies visit the campus for recruitment, we conduct company-specific training programs to prepare students for the hiring process. These training sessions include:

  • Understanding company profiles, job roles, and expectations
  • Mock interviews and group discussions
  • Technical assessments and coding practice
  • HR and behavioral training

5. Placement Opportunities Across Multiple Sectors

We actively connect students with job opportunities across various industries, including:

  • Information Technology & Software Development
  • Core Engineering & Manufacturing
  • Banking, Finance & Consulting
  • Healthcare & Pharmaceuticals
  • E-commerce & Digital Marketing
